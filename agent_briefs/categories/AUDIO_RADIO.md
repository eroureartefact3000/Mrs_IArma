# Category brief — Audio & Radio Lions

> Audio-only creative. Self-contained.

---

## What Audio & Radio celebrates

Creative work delivered through **audio only**: radio spots, podcasts, audio-led campaigns, sound-design-led branded content. Must land **without visual support**: voice, music, sound design, timing.

**Important note for extraction**: boards in this category are CASE STUDIES of the audio work, not the audio itself. Extract from keyframes, transcript snippets, production credits. Set `review_flag: true` if the board lacks audio evidence (waveform, script excerpt, credits).

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: **a TV film with the audio extracted** and submitted as radio.

How to detect: does the audio stand on its own? Is there a script that builds tension via voice + sound? Or is it just a 30s of dialog ripped from a film?

When the work fails the native check, hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50.

## Patterns commonly seen in Audio winners

| Pattern | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| `voiceover-as-the-idea` | A piece of writing that lives in the voice (Geico, John Lewis) |
| `sound-effect-as-mnemonic` | A signature sound that becomes the brand cue |
| `audio-illusion` | Stereo / 3D audio tricks that surprise the listener |
| `music-as-product-demo` | Original composition that demonstrates the product attribute |
| `podcast-as-brand-platform` | Recurring branded podcast with real listenership |
| `silence-as-craft` | Negative space, breath, restraint |
