# Category brief — Entertainment Lions for Sport

> Brand-entertainment work built around sport. Self-contained.

---

## What Entertainment Lions for Sport celebrate

Creative work where **sport is the brand-entertainment vehicle** — sponsorship-led storytelling, athlete partnerships, branded content tied to sport, sport-as-platform for cultural moments. The work must be **genuinely entertaining**, not just sport-themed advertising.

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: **sport-themed advertising masquerading as entertainment**.

Common offenders:
- A **30-second TV spot** with a famous athlete cameo — not entertainment, just an ad
- A **sponsorship logo placement** with no creative content built around it
- A **branded content piece** that has nothing actually entertaining (it's an explainer)
- A **PR-only campaign** that picked up sport media but had no entertainment artefact

How to detect:
- Is there a **content artefact** the audience chose to watch/consume?
- Does the content **stand on its own** as entertainment, even if you stripped the brand?
- Is sport the **platform**, not just the backdrop?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50 in the rationale.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to Entertainment for Sport but executed as a 30-second sponsorship ad — there's no actual entertainment artefact for the jury to reward."*

## Patterns in Sport-Entertainment winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `athlete-as-narrator` | Athlete's voice drives a brand documentary or content series |
| `sponsorship-inversion` | Brand sponsors an unexpected sport / athlete / moment |
| `live-moment-content` | Real-time content responding to a live sport event |
| `behind-the-scenes-doc` | Long-form documentary on athletes / teams enabled by brand sponsorship |
| `fandom-celebration` | Content celebrating the sport's fan culture, not the sport itself |
| `social-cause-via-sport` | Sport platform used to advance a social issue (Adidas Concha, Mastercard Wheelchair Ballkid) |
| `partnership-unlock` | Brand × league deal unlocks unique content rights |
