# Category brief — Social & Influencer Lions

> Social-native creative + creator partnerships. Self-contained.

---

## What Social & Influencer celebrates

Creative work that uses **social platforms and influencer partnerships** — community management, real-time response, influencer-led campaigns, social content series, creator partnerships. The work must be **native to the platform**, not repurposed advertising.

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: **a TV-style ad cut to vertical and posted on TikTok**.

Common offenders:
- A **repurposed film campaign** chopped into social formats
- An **influencer post** that's really a paid placement with no creative integration
- A **digital banner campaign** badged as social
- A **community management exercise** with no real community engagement (vanity metrics only)

How to detect:
- Is the work **format-native** (would not work on TV, would not work as a poster)?
- Is the creator's **voice preserved** (not just a brand script read by the creator)?
- Is there **audience-participation evidence** (UGC, dialogue, challenges) — not just impressions?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50 in the rationale.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to Social but executed as a paid-influencer placement with no creative integration — the jury had no social-native creative to reward."*

## Patterns in Social winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `creator-led-format-invention` | Creator invents a content format the brand co-owns |
| `real-time-cultural-hijack` | Brand responds to a live cultural moment within hours |
| `community-managed-conversation` | Community management craft (Wendy's Twitter, Aldi UK) |
| `UGC-as-the-campaign` | Audience contribution IS the work (TikTok challenges, hashtag campaigns) |
| `brand-fan-relationship` | Long-running brand-fan dialogue building affinity |
| `platform-feature-exploitation` | Native use of a platform feature (Instagram filters, X polls, Reddit AMA) |
| `behaviour-change-via-social` | Social campaign driving a documented behaviour shift |
