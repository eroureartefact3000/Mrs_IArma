# Category brief — Health & Wellness Lions

> Per-category specifics. Consumer-facing health work (NOT regulated Pharma). Self-contained.

---

## What Health & Wellness celebrates

Work for **consumer-facing health and wellness** — OTC products, healthcare services, wellbeing brands, behavioural-change campaigns aimed at general audiences. Distinguished from Pharma (regulated prescription). The work should **educate, motivate, or shift health behaviour with genuine human care**.

## Native-to-category check

Health's failure mode: **clinical-tone work with no real human empathy**, OR **purpose-led work with no real health credentials**.

Common offenders:
- A **brand-purpose campaign** that touches health tangentially (more suited to Glass / SDG)
- A **patient-as-prop** campaign where real patients are objects, not subjects
- An **overstated benefit claim** that wouldn't survive medical scrutiny
- A **scaremongering ad** that motivates through fear rather than empowerment

How to detect:
- Is the **health insight** rooted in real behavioural or clinical truth?
- Does the brand have **legitimate authority** to make the health claim?
- Is the tone **respectful** to the patient / consumer — neither saccharine nor scaremongering?
- Is there documented **health-behaviour change** (not just impressions)?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50 in the rationale.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to Health but executed as a purpose-led campaign with cosmetic health framing — the jury had no documented behaviour change to reward."*

## Patterns in Health winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `screening-rate-shift` | Specific increase in testing / screening behaviour |
| `taboo-breaking-empathy` | Bringing a stigmatised condition into the open (mental health, sexual health) |
| `tech-as-health-utility` | App / device / service that delivers real health benefit |
| `community-as-co-author` | Patient community shapes the campaign voice |
| `data-led-public-health` | Original health data generating earned coverage |
| `behaviour-change-mechanism` | Specific call-to-action driving documented health behaviour |
| `inclusive-health-design` | Health work designed for under-served populations |

## Checklist

- [ ] Strategy stays strictly WHY (brand health credentials, audience insight, cultural sensitivity)
- [ ] Execution stays strictly HOW (craft, tonality, channel fit)
- [ ] Native check applied — Health needs real empathy + real credentials
- [ ] **Health-behaviour outcome named** if claimed (screening rate, vaccination, lifestyle shift)
- [ ] RIGOUR patterns 1-5 applied
- [ ] No scaremongering or condescension in the rationale
- [ ] Weighted score = 0.25·Idea + 0.20·Strategy + 0.25·Execution + 0.30·Impact
