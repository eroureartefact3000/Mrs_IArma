# Category brief — Creative Strategy Lions

> **Signal caveat**: Creative Strategy is a strategy-first category. Boards are typically text-heavy case studies of the strategic thinking. Visual signal will be weaker than visual categories. Self-contained.

---

## What Creative Strategy celebrates

Work that celebrates **the strategic thinking that unlocked creative work** — the insight, audience definition, brand positioning, business reframe, or cultural framing that gave the creative its power. The board is a strategy case study; the creative execution is downstream evidence.

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: **work that's submitted because the agency couldn't win in another category**.

Common offenders:
- A **PR-led campaign** rebadged as strategy
- An **awareness campaign** with no specific strategic insight
- An **execution-heavy campaign** with thin underlying thinking
- A **strategy document** with no actual creative or business outcome

How to detect:
- Is there a **named strategic insight** on the board (the "aha" that unlocked the brief)?
- Is the insight **defensible** (data-rooted, not just plausible)?
- Did the strategy **produce a measurable business outcome** that traces back to the insight?
- Is the creative work **proof** of the strategy or just decoration?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Strategy ≤ 50 in the rationale (the entire category's reason to exist).

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to Creative Strategy but no specific strategic insight is named on the board — the work reads as a creative campaign with strategy framing applied retrospectively."*

## Patterns in Creative Strategy winners

| Pattern | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| `audience-reframe` | Redefining who the target audience is, opening new business |
| `brief-flip` | Inverting the original brief (sell less, slow down, etc.) |
| `cultural-insight-anchor` | A cultural truth that unlocks broad-base behaviour change |
| `data-as-the-insight` | Proprietary research generating a strategic-led campaign |
| `business-problem-reframe` | Turning a competitive disadvantage into the brand's asset |
| `behavioural-economics-applied` | Documented behavioural insight (loss aversion, social proof, framing) |
| `cross-category-insight-application` | Insight from one industry applied creatively to another |

## Checklist

- [ ] **Specific strategic insight named** in Strategy rationale (THE thing that unlocked everything)
- [ ] Insight assessed for veracity (RIGOUR pattern 3 — data-rooted, not assumption)
- [ ] Strategy ↔ creative idea coherence assessed in Idea rationale
- [ ] Execution kept light (10% weight — don't over-analyse craft here)
- [ ] Business outcome assessed in Impact rationale
- [ ] RIGOUR patterns 1-5 applied
- [ ] Synthesis observational
- [ ] Weighted score = 0.20·Idea + 0.50·Strategy + 0.10·Execution + 0.20·Impact
