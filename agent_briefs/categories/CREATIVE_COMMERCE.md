## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: a brand-awareness campaign with a token "buy now" CTA and no documented sales outcome.

How to detect: is there a **named commerce KPI** (sales lift, conversion %, new customers acquired, AOV) on the board? Does the mechanism create friction-free path to purchase, or just generate impressions?

When the native check fails, hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Impact ≤ 50.

## Patterns in Commerce winners

| Pattern | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| `media-as-checkout` | The ad IS the buy button (shoppable media, scannable OOH) |
| `livestream-commerce` | Live-streamed product launches with real-time conversion |
| `cultural-moment-to-sale` | Riding a cultural beat into measurable sales lift |
| `subscription-as-product` | Recurring commerce model reinvented |
| `marketplace-hack` | Smart use of Amazon, TikTok Shop, retail apps as creative canvas |
| `data-driven-personalisation` | One-to-one offers driving disproportionate response |
