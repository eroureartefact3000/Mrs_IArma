# Category brief — Innovation Lions

> Technological or methodological breakthrough. Self-contained.

## Axis weights
Idea 0.30 · Strategy 0.10 · Execution 0.30 · Impact 0.30
`weighted_score = 0.30·I + 0.10·S + 0.30·E + 0.30·R`

## Tier bands
GP 90-100 · Gold 80-100 · Silver 65-80 · Bronze 50-65

## Sub-categories
A. Applied Innovation · B. Use of AI/ML · C. Use of Emerging Tech (XR, blockchain) · D. Industry Innovation

## Native-to-category check
Failure mode: brand campaign that mentions "we used AI" without any actual technical innovation. Detect: is there a **technical first** documented (engineering achievement, patent, novel methodology)? Or just buzzword usage? Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 if just buzzword.

## Patterns
`technical-first-applied-creatively`, `AI-as-creative-medium`, `XR-as-product-demo`, `methodology-innovation`, `accessibility-tech-breakthrough`, `platform-feature-invented`.
