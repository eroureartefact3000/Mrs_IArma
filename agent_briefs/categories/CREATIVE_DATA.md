## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: a campaign that mentions "we used data" but the work itself is generic.

How to detect: is there a **specific data insight** that drives the creative? Was the data proprietary or original research? Or is it generic CRM usage rebadged as Creative Data?

When the native check fails, hard-cap Strategy ≤ 50 (the entire reason this category exists).

| Pattern | What it looks like |
