# Category brief — Sustainable Development Goals Lions

> Creative work advancing one or more UN SDGs. Impact-driven. Self-contained.

---

## What SDG celebrates

Work that **demonstrably advances one of the UN's 17 SDGs** — not just borrows purpose. The defining test: did this work actually move a development metric (people fed, vaccinated, schooled, employed, protected)?

## Native-to-category check

SDG's failure mode: **purpose-washing** — work that touches a development theme cosmetically but doesn't move any metric.

Common offenders:
- A **brand-awareness campaign** with an SDG tagline
- A **donation match** with no documented donation outcome
- A **theme-of-the-month** opportunistic campaign (Women's Day, Earth Day) with no follow-through
- A **purpose-led ad** running once, with no documented audience action

How to detect:
- Is there a **specific SDG named** (e.g. "SDG 5: Gender Equality") or just generic 'good cause'?
- Does the board show **documented outcomes** tied to that SDG?
- Did the brand have **authority** to speak (real CSR programme, existing partnerships)?
- Is the impact narrative believable at scale?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Impact ≤ 50 in the rationale and call it out.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to SDG but executed as a brand-awareness campaign with cosmetic purpose framing — no documented SDG metric was moved."*

## Patterns in SDG winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `legislative-shift` | Campaign explicitly designed to move a law / policy (UN Women Child Wedding Cards) |
| `documented-behaviour-change` | Specific population-level shift in a measurable behaviour |
| `branded-utility-for-SDG` | Brand offers a service/tool that delivers SDG progress (free education app, water-saving device) |
| `data-newsmaking-for-SDG` | Original research generating SDG-themed earned coverage |
| `community-as-co-author` | Affected community shapes the campaign voice (not brand speaking for them) |
| `partnership-with-NGO/multilateral` | Brand × UN agency / major NGO unlocking joint impact |
| `infrastructure-delivered` | Real infrastructure built/delivered (wells, schools, clinics) |

## Checklist

- [ ] Strategy stays strictly WHY

- [ ] **Documented SDG metric moved** assessed in Impact rationale
- [ ] RIGOUR patterns 1-5 applied
- [ ] No vague claims like "raised awareness" without behaviour shift
