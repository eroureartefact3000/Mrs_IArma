# Category brief — Entertainment Lions

> Brand-entertainment work outside Sport/Music/Gaming (which have dedicated Lions). Self-contained.

---

## What Entertainment celebrates

Creative work delivering brand messages through **genuinely entertaining content** — branded films, content series, podcasts, branded utility entertainment, gaming, live shows. The work must **earn audience attention**, not interrupt it. Distinct from the dedicated Entertainment Lions for Music / Sport / Gaming.

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: **branded content that nobody would watch without the brand**.

Common offenders:
- A **30-second TV spot** with a famous talent cameo — not entertainment
- A **brand documentary** that's really a product explainer
- A **promotional video** dressed up as content
- A **PR-only campaign** that generated content press but had no entertainment artefact

How to detect:
- **Strip the brand** mentally — is there still a piece of entertainment people would consume?
- Did the content **earn its audience** (opt-in views, completion rates) or buy it (paid placements)?
- Is there a **content vehicle** (film, series, podcast, live show) the jury can name?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50 in the rationale.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to Entertainment but executed as a brand explainer dressed as content — there's no entertainment artefact the audience would seek out independently."*

## Patterns in Entertainment winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `branded-franchise` | Brand creates a recurring entertainment franchise (Mailchimp Email Is Dead, Apple "Shot On") |
| `behind-the-scenes-doc` | Long-form documentary on a topic the brand has authority over |
| `creator-collaboration` | Brand × creator deal unlocking creator-led content with brand fit |
| `cultural-icon-recontextualisation` | Famous IP / icon repurposed for brand storytelling |
| `branded-podcast` | Long-form audio that earns repeat listenership |
| `interactive-entertainment` | Choose-your-own-adventure, AR/VR, live participatory shows |
| `social-content-series` | TikTok / Reels series that builds an audience over time |
