# Category brief — Luxury & Lifestyle Lions

> Creative work for luxury / premium lifestyle brands. Self-contained.

## Axis weights
Idea 0.25 · Strategy 0.15 · Execution 0.35 · Impact 0.25
`weighted_score = 0.25·I + 0.15·S + 0.35·E + 0.25·R`

## Tier bands
GP 90-100 · Gold 80-100 · Silver 65-80 · Bronze 50-65

## Sub-categories
A. Sectors (Fashion, Jewellery, Watches, Beauty, Hotels, Auto, Spirits) · B. Excellence in Luxury Craft · C. Cultural Engagement in Luxury

## Native-to-category check
Failure mode: mass-market brand campaign submitted as luxury because of high production budget. Detect: does the work have **luxury tonal precision** (restraint, cultural depth, heritage tension)? Or is it loud and mass-targeted? Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 if tonal mismatch.

## Patterns
`heritage-modernised`, `restraint-as-craft`, `artist-collab-luxury`, `cultural-icon-elevation`, `materiality-as-the-point`, `audience-precision-narrow`.

## Checklist
- [ ] Strategy strictly WHY (brand heritage + luxury audience)

- [ ] Native check applied — **tonal fit assessed**
- [ ] Cultural conversation / brand-equity shift named in Impact
- [ ] RIGOUR patterns 1-5 applied
