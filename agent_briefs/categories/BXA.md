# Category brief — Brand Experience & Activation Lions

> Per-category specifics for Cannes Lions BXA: axis weights, sub-category landscape, judging emphasis. Self-contained.

---

## What BXA celebrates

Brand Experience & Activation Lions celebrate work that **brings brands to life through experiences** — activations, sponsorships, retail, partnerships, live events. The defining test: did people actually **participate**, not just see?

Weights are balanced: experiential is BOTH craft-driven AND outcome-driven.

## Native-to-category check

BXA's failure mode: **a film campaign with a one-off photo of an event**, submitted as experiential.

Common offenders:
- A **case film** of an "event" with no documented participation
- A **PR stunt** with a single photographable moment but no audience experience
- A **digital activation** with no physical or live component (often belongs in Direct or Social)
- A **paid sponsorship** with no creative use of the partnership rights

How to detect:
- Look for **actual participation numbers** — how many people attended, played, voted, queued?
- Is there an **experience design** the audience interacts with, or just a posed image?
- Did the activation **generate earned media** because of what people did, not what the brand said?

When the work fails the native check:
- Hard-cap Idea ≤ 60 and Execution ≤ 50 in the rationale and call it out.

Example phrasing:

> *"Submitted to BXA but executed as a case film with a single posed event photo — the jury had no documented participation experience to reward."*

## Patterns in BXA winners

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| `participation-becomes-the-news` | Activation drives such mass participation that the participation itself becomes news |
| `branded-utility-experience` | Brand offers a useful service or tool people opt into for real |
| `sponsorship-inversion` | Brand sponsors something unexpected, redefining the partnership rules |
| `live-moment-as-stunt` | One-time live moment designed to be photographed/filmed/talked about |
| `retail-as-experience` | Store / pop-up / installation that's an experience, not a transaction |
| `cultural-festival-takeover` | Brand owns a cultural moment (festival, sports event) creatively |
| `behaviour-change-activation` | Activation drives a specific behaviour with documented before/after |
