## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: a creative case study with impressive-looking metrics but no attribution methodology and no control group.

How to detect:
- Is there **longitudinal data** (months / years, not just launch period)?
- Is there **attribution methodology** explained? (control group, time series, regression, etc.)
- Are the metrics **business-grade** (revenue, market share, lifetime value) not vanity (impressions, reach)?

When the native check fails, hard-cap Impact ≤ 50.
