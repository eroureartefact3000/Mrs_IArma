# Category brief — Film Craft Lions

> Craft-dominant. Direction, cinematography, editing, sound, music, casting, VFX. **Signal caveat**: boards are case studies; real work is the film. Self-contained.

## Native-to-category check

Failure mode: a generic brand film with workmanlike production submitted as Film Craft.

How to detect: does the board show **specific craft achievement** (DP credit, named director with festival pedigree, signature lighting style, identifiable editorial rhythm)? Or just keyframes from a 30-second spot?

When the native check fails, hard-cap Idea ≤ 50 and Execution ≤ 55.

---

## Patterns in Film Craft winners

| Pattern | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| `single-take-craft` | Long takes / oners as the technical achievement (Spike Jonze, Romain Gavras) |
| `director-as-the-idea` | Auteur director's signature carries the work (Wes Anderson Bouygues, etc.) |
| `lighting-as-narrative` | DP work that tells the emotional story |
| `casting-as-the-idea` | Performance carries everything (Adobe Boy & Kite, Apple Welcome Home) |
| `sound-craft-as-the-point` | Score / sound design is THE achievement |
| `VFX-invisible-but-essential` | Photo-real VFX that supports rather than dominates |
